## Bosses

### Wall of Death

A long hallway with a monster or scary-magic-thing of some kind on one end and an exit on the other.

You have to run along the hallway, solving golem-time traps and puzzles to get to the exit. Meanwhile, the wall encroaches on you in real-time.

The pace of this match is faster than typical real-time. He comes at you quick.

This could be the first real boss encountered. The game starts with turn-based golem time, then does a shock-twist reveal about its real-time, so this boss would be a fun play on both concepts and could represent your final challenge before leaving the game's beginning "golem trial zone".

## Other Ideas

### An Illusion that Postures

A mirage, if you will, that disappears when the illusion is violated, i.e., when it touches you.

For that reason, it focuses on intimidation, and only attacks with powerful and scary blasts and blows when it knows it would miss. If it ever actually makes contact, you'd realize immediately that it isn't real, and it would become dust.
