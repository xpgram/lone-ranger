# Abilities

## Abilities

### Move

Move self to adjacent tiles.

### Push

Move adjacent objects one tile over.

### Shove

An upgrade to Push: allows you to move up to 3 objects in a line at once.

'Soft' objects, like fleshy enemies, can still be crushed by stones pushed onto them.

### Wings

Allows you to float over 1 void tile.

### Hammer

Cracks / destroys various objects.

Can kill / annoy enemies.

### Hookshot

Zoinks you to a new location, 5-tiles away.

Can only target hookable materials.

Light enemies / objects may be pulled toward you instead.

### Shield?

Allows you to withstand certain attacks.

Some enemies will be easier to deal with.

Some traps, like danger streams (think 'fire spout'), will be newly navigable.

## Spells

### Burn

A simple spell like this is a good idea to introduce the magic system. Players can imagine attacking something with fire, and we can mix in the synergies concept from there.

Players collect Burn. They may try using it on the first golem enemies. Those are stone, so it won't work. We begin by learning about Burn through its other effects.

*Later,* they'll see fleshy enemies. If they're clever enough to try using Burn again, they'll discover it *can* be used the way they first thought, but that they need to pay attention to a target's *properties*.

- Burn flammable enemies / materials.

- Light fuel / gas / etc.

- Multiple uses can melt metals / rocks.

- An explosion-style spell that affects at least a 2x2 area.
  - If I make this wider, this could expand from the cast point via a reach-like system. That is, the explosion would have to travel *around* walls, which means it could combo with *Raise*.
  - *Explosion* could also be a separate spell.


### Shock

- Chain to nearby targets, if they're conductive.
  - This doesn't mean each target is damaged, but it can be a way to reach far away targets.
- Paralyze certain enemies, giving you time to fuff about.
- Energize / overclock certain machine-like targets.

### Needle

- Fires in a straight line, in any of the four facing directions.
- Ricochets sometimes off of objects / enemies.
  - This would require chirality, either a left-spin or a right-spin, and that would determine which ricochet direction is chosen when it makes contact with an enemy.
  - I could have two chiral spells (eh), or I could have a spell-mod button of some kind during casting. For convenience, this would probably be best used *during* the target selection screen, not in the command menu.
- This spell could be made more useful if there were range limitations to the typical area-cast spell.
  - For example, even though it may be within 4 tiles, *Fire* could not be cast 'through a wall'.
  - *Fire* would be constrained by a reach-like system similar to movement in Advance Wars.

### Crush

- Compactifies things.
  - Loose particulates can be compactified into a new floor tile.
- Increase atmospheric pressure.

### Crack

- Weaken/destroy enemy armor, then hit them with something else.
- Break floor tiles, which then behave like glass; step over them, leaving enemies behind a gap.
  - No soft-lock: you can fall in yourself, which will take you back to a labyrinth checkpoint.

### Raise

- Turn a floor tile into a weak wall. (Crack or Hammer to destroy)
  - Soft-locking: these should crumble over time?
- Block enemies / traps / lines of sight.
- Can lift objects to an upper floor?
- Can lift levers to an up position from far away?

### Float

- Floated objects are not harmed by flooring.
- This is getting a bit Baba Is You.
- I dunno if I like this one; it feels cliché. I need a clever twist.

### Protect

- Puts a barrier around a tile or a thing that can survive 1-or-so attacks.
- You can put it on yourself, then touch an enemy.
- You could put it on an enemy you need, then let them touch spikes or whatever.
- In Frieren, they put a barrier around Stark to stop a wolf bite, and while lodged in the wolf's mouth, he attacked the mouth. Big axe swing. It was cool.
  - Maybe the Bull gets dazed when colliding with objects, but this is brief and hard to take advantage of. Protect lets him bump into you (without flinching from pain) so you can respond immediately.
    Oh, I guess 'dazed' in this case means 'lowers his armor'. The bulls would need armor.

### Rhino

- Not a real suggestion, just a thought.
- Makes the target charge forward.
  - Turns taken are also faster than the normal turn system. They move independently every 1.5 seconds or so.
- Puzzle example:
  - A button is in the middle of a platform. This button extends a 2-tile bridge between two platforms.
  - An enemy is on one platform and should be on the other.
  - Normally, the enemy either will not approach the bridge, or trying to pass turns for it to move would require stepping off the button, retracting the bridge and letting the enemy fall.
  - Solution: change the enemy's faced direction somehow, hold the button down, cast *Rhino* on them, wait for them to cross the bridge.
- If this were a real suggestion, what if this spell was drawn from the Bull enemy somehow?
  - In FF8, you could draw magic from the field or from enemies in battle. Just a thought.

- Illuminates dark or invisible things.

### Void

## Items

### Vial

Of something.

- Heals 2 hearts.

### Dial

- Slows the passage of time. Enemies get free turns less often.

### Stimulant

- Lowers all (or most?) action time costs to 75%. The player sometimes gets free turns.

### Sync

- Makes all real-time objects operate in golem time.

## Traps

### Bomb

Blows up 3x3 area.

Triggerable instantly with fire.

Blows up other bombs struck by this one.

### Bomb (proximity)

Arms first when you step near it.

Sometimes buried or hard to see until you arm it.

### Caustic Weather

Like Metroid Prime Echoes, you gotta hop between safe zones.

### Hidden Flooring

Looks like a Pit normally, but when illuminated with light, you can see glimmers of its edges.

*Burn* could leave a burning flame that illuminates its surrounding area for a time.
