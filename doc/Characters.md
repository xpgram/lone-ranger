# Characters

### Pop Idol

Based on the Teto song "Spoken For". Like the Master of Faces (whatever that folklore monster is called), she takes on the personality of whatever you imagine her to be. Or rather, whichever narrative.

If you meet her and think she's a funny pop-idol, then she's a funny pop-idol. If you learn something and think she's sinister or conniving, then she's sinister and conniving. I see her personality as a kind of **cold reading**, where her personal history is actually decided by your perception of her and not her actual life.

### Creepy Twins

Kinda like Nurse Joy? In... spirit, I guess. Probably not as cute.

They're actually puppets that a single consciousness flits between.

There's a scene later where you meet one of them again, but the consciousness is not occupying them yet, so they're just sitting there staring at nothing as if they're in a standing coma. You try to get their attention several times while they stare past you, glassy eyed, until they suddenly perk up and greet you cheerily as if nothing had happened.
